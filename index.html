PRATISHTHA BLOG

Pratishtha: The Heartbeat of Our College Pratishtha, our annual college fest, is a vibrant celebration of talent, creativity, and determination. It is divided into three dynamic sections:
1. Verve - The cultural fest that showcases artistic expression through music, dance, drama, and more. Verve is where creativity comes alive, leaving audiences inspired.
2. Aurum - The tech fest that fuels innovation with hackathons, tech showcases, and coding competitions. Its a hub for tech enthusiasts to turn ideas into reality.
3. Olympus - The sports fest that celebrates athleticism and teamwork. From thrilling matches to adrenaline-pumping races, Olympus is all about the spirit of competition.
Pratishtha is more than just an event—its a celebration of unity and excellence that brings our college together.
